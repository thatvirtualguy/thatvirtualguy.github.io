Welcome back!
My name is Ado, aka [That Virtual Guy](/) and I used to be a web developer startnig my career in 2007.

I made a careerswitch in 2014 and became a [virtual assistant](../vas/what-is-a-va/), working from my home office for various companies and individuals. In the past 2.5 years I am customer support agent for a call center from Austria.

I'm passionate about helping people, both as a personal assistant to my wife who is a wheelchair user as well as a customer support agent for my clients.

We have a simple mission: helping as many stay at home parents to provide for their family. We want to teach you about the ins and outs, dos and don'ts of the VA business. Learn how to write your CV the right way, what skills you need and land your first client. Become a fully booked VA! 

[Debunkz](../tags/debunkz/) are website reviews for our reades. Finding hundreds & thousands posts daily about "amazing websites where you can earn hundreds of $/day" we want to take a deeper look at those websites. Living in Bosnia and Herzegovina, not all of these website are available to people in third world countries. We show you the website, what services you can do and after a week of usage the results how much money we earned after a week of work.

Although AI is new on the market, we will strive to find the best [AI companions](../tags/aicompanions/) for our readers, making their daily work easier. We strongly believe that AI will not take over our virtual assistant places, but rather be a every day helper.

We strongly believe that [Web3](../tags/web3/) and especially cryptocurrencies have one of the biggest potentials to become a steady stream of income by creating quality content.
